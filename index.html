From a very early age, I was always building things and breaking toys open to figure out how they were built and what else I could invent with the parts. Following my passion and curiosity, I earned a degree in Mechanical Engineering which gave me the foundation for being able to understand complex problems and come up with creative solutions.

I spent years exploring software and the web, but it wasn’t until I ran operations at a small startup that I became fully aware of my passion for software development and the impact my contribution can have to a company and to the community. I believe that software should be practical and improve our lives in a meaningful way. I’m excited to work with an amazing team that is like minded and can help build the future with me one amazing product at a time.
